Redeemer's Grace Church Missionaries

We currently support 20+ missionaries all throughout the world!

Our vision at RGC is to know and be involved in what God is doing in as many parts of the world as possible. We pray for our missionaries regularly through Missions Matters, following up to know how we can be praying specifically for them. Paul's ambition in Romans 15 is to preach Christ where He has not yet been named, and we want to join that same bold advance through prayer.
